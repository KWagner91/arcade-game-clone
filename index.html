Frogger Arcade Game Clone

In this game, you must cross the street avoiding the evil men-eating ladybugs and reach the water side.

You can navigate, using the arrow keys on your keyboard. To change to a different character sprite, press "C".

This game has 5 levels, after passing level 5, the game ends. Beware, more ladybugs are waiting to eat you with each level and they become faster!

Game Design and Functionality by Kerstin Wagner
